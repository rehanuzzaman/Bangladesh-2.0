Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving progress through creativity, technology, and forward-thinking solutions for future.

Sustainability

Balancing development with environmental preservation for our upcoming generations.

Infrastructure

Building robust systems to connect and support communities and economies for a sustainable future.

Empowerment

The Vision of Future

The vision of the future embraces a world where innovation fuels sustainable growth, bridging humanity with cutting-edge technology. It envisions harmony between progress and the environment, fostering a planet that thrives alongside its inhabitants. Empowered communities lead the way, breaking barriers through education, equity, and collaboration. Infrastructure transforms into a backbone of connectivity, inclusivity, and resilience. Together, we shape a brighter tomorrow, where every individual contributes to a shared global prosperity.

Digital Transformation

Revolutionizing industries and daily life through advanced technology and seamless connectivity.

Green Energy

Championing renewable resources to power a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building innovative, resilient systems that support growth and connectivity in a changing world.

"I believe that we can create a poverty-free world because poverty is not created by poor people. It has been created and sustained by the economic and social systems that we have designed for ourselves; the institutions and concepts that make up that system; the policies that we pursue.
To overcome poverty and the flaws of the economic crisis in our society, we need to envision our social life. We have to free our mind, imagine what has never happened before and write social fiction. We need to imagine things to make them happen. If you don't imagine, it will never happen."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Advisor of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
